Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can we go live?

Most businesses are deployed in 20 minutes or less. You create your account, add users, and assign training.

What if we do not have SOPs documented?

No problem. You can start immediately with the Essentials Library. Later, you can add your SOPs, build proprietary modules, and tailor role-based paths to your workflow, or have us do it for you through the White Glove Rollout.

Do you build the training for us?

Yes. Through the White Glove Rollout, we convert your employee handbook, processes, and workflows into a branded, trackable training experience with quizzes, acknowledgments, and role-based learning paths.

What happens in the monthly manager meeting?

Managers review training activity, completion, and reporting from the system, then set the focus and standards for the next month using the monthly focus plan.

Can we train different roles differently?

Yes. You can create role-based learning paths and tailor training to match each role’s responsibilities and workflow.
